Staff & Student Accounting

Accounting of students and employees is a three step sequential procedure to ensure all students and employees are accounted for. Any time students and staff are moved to a fire drill location, Staff-Student Staging Area, and/or Reunification Site, or a Shelter-in-Place or Hard or Soft Lockdown is enacted, initiate the Staff and Student Accounting Protocol.

Action Steps:

Teacher/Staff Must Respond by:

  • Taking attendance visually (see) and auditorily (by name) accounting for each student on the class roster and obtaining a count of the students in your care.

Evacuation:

  • Display teacher identification sign at the fire drill location, Staff-Student Staging Area, and/or Reunification Site.
  • Attach Green Card (all present) or Red Card (assistance needed).
  • Label each student, if continuing on to Family Reunification site.
  • If no students, report to Staff Coordinator for appropriate assignment.
