Reverse Evacuation

Reverse Evacuation Protocol is used when staff and students who are outside of the school facility must be moved as quickly as possible into the school facility for protection. This action is done when there is a potential threat or danger outside of the facility or when a Soft Lockdown or Shelter-In-Place is called. Reverse Evacuation may also be used for a Hard Lockdown if the threat is outside the school.
